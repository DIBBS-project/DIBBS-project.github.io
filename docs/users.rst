User authentication
===================

On the first access you will be presented the following screen which states that
you need to authenticate. Click on the **authenticate** button to be redirected
on the Central Authentication Service.

.. image:: images/auth_1.png

On the following screen you will be asked to enter your credentials. If you
don't have any credentials yet, you have to request the creation of an account
by clicking on the **sign up** link.

.. image:: images/auth_2.png

The following screen depicts the process of requesting the creation of a new
account on a DIBBS platform. After the submission of this form, you will be
redirected to the previous screen and you will be able to sign-in.

.. image:: images/auth_3.png

Managing OpenStack credentials
==============================

The DIBBS platform needs to have access to some OpenStack credentials in order
to spawn computing resources that will host the execution of operations.

To access to the credential management page, click on your username on the
top-right corner, and then click on the **Credentials** button as depicted in
the following screen:

.. image:: images/user_1.png

You will land on the following screen which enables to see the credentials you
already registered in the DIBBS platform.

.. image:: images/user_2.png

To add new credentials to the DIBBS platform, please click on the **+ Add
Credentials** located in the top-right corner. You will land on the following
form:

.. image:: images/user_3.png
